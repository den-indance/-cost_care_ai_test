# CostCare AI - Healthcare Cost Optimization Platform

## What is CostCare AI?

CostCare AI is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to help healthcare providers, hospitals, and medical facilities optimize their operational costs while maintaining or improving the quality of patient care. Our platform uses machine learning algorithms to analyze spending patterns, identify inefficiencies, and provide actionable recommendations.
